Geeksup

GeeksUp aims to identifies and empowers the ambitious individual from Tier-2 and Tier-3 cities across the nation and provide the platform to leverage their skills. We introduce various programs for exposing them to a world of opportunities. They interact with leading technologists around the world getting mentored on technical and business skills. We designed a carefully curated series of programmes to accomplish this goal.
